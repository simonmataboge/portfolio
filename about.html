More about me

I was born at Madidi then moved to Klipgat, I attended school at Tlapa la thuto primary school.At grade 2 I was nominated to attend a growth program at Nampak Rosslyn, that is where I saw a laptop for the first time and I instantly fell in love with it and I told the user and myself that when I grow up, I want to use it
Unfortunately township schools did not have resources for such skills at that time so I had to put it on hold and utilize what I had to survive. In 2000 I moved to Letlhabile. Letlhabile is a place where I discovered the love and the skills of Information Technology. I studied at Molelwaneng Primary School.
I went to Tshepagalang Secondary School where I matriculated and I was honoured with certificate for top achiever of maths standard grade After matriculating I was forced to start working since I couldn't further my studies as a reason. It was the right choice at the time
I went to work for Parmalat SA as a merchandiser then I was promoted to field marketer after two years.Several years later I was nominated for the in-store awards but unfortunately I did not win. Being nominated from a small town like Brits, was more of a win for me
