Cookies are small pieces of a text sent to web browser by a website you visit. The file is ussually stored in your web browser and alloe services or a third party to recognise you.Persistent Cookies remain on your device when you go offline, while session Cookies are deleted as soon as you close your browser

We use cookies for the following:

  • To provide analytics
  • To store your preferences
  • To enable advertisements
  • To enable certain functions of the service

Types of cookies

  • Essential cookies: Are used to remember information that changes the way service behaves or looks
  • Accounts-related cookies: Used to authenticate users, may be used for services such as remember me functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Used to track information how the service can be improved may also be used to test new advertisements
  • Advertising cookies: Are used to deliver adverts and track performence of these adverts. They may also be used to enable third-partyadvertising networks to deliver ads that might be relevent to your preferences

Please Note: If you delete or refuse to accept cookies, you might not be able to use all features we offer
